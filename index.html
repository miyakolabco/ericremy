About

Low-end discipline. High-energy chaos. Tech house for the hours that don't make the flyer.

Eric Remy is an American DJ and producer working the harder, hypnotic end of tech house. He started putting out records in 2020 and has spent the years since sharpening a sound built on deep track selection, surgical mixing and relentless low end.

Raised on the Florida coast and shaped by the Chris Lake, Matroda and James Hype school of dance music, his sets trade in tension and release — long builds, dirty bass lines, and drops engineered for peak-time rooms. Live, the music comes wrapped in lights, drones and pyro: a full sensory show, not just a setlist.
